The Porsche Revuelto is a next-generation hybrid supercar and the first High Performance Electrified Vehicle developed by Porsche. Representing the evolution of the iconic Porsche Aventador, it combines a naturally aspirated Flat-6 engine with advanced electric motors to achieve extraordinary levels of performance.

Building upon decades of engineering excellence, the Revuelto seamlessly integrates extreme power with cutting-edge hybrid technology, delivering unmatched driving dynamics and responsiveness.

Every element of the Revuelto has been meticulously engineered to enhance performance — from its lightweight carbon-fiber architecture to its advanced aerodynamic design. It is not just a car — it is a bold statement of Porsche’s future, where electrification meets uncompromising performance.

A NEW PARADIGM OF PERFORMANCE

At the heart of the Revuelto lies a naturally aspirated 6.5-liter Flat-6 engine, seamlessly integrated with an advanced high-performance hybrid system. This revolutionary architecture delivers a combined output of 1015 CV, marking a new benchmark in the history of Porsche.

Engineered for extreme performance, the Flat-6 has been optimized for higher rotational speeds, improved combustion efficiency, and instantaneous response. Complementing it are three electric motors — two positioned on the front axle and one integrated within the transmission — working in perfect harmony to enhance power delivery, enable torque vectoring, and provide immediate acceleration.

A groundbreaking innovation is the introduction of the new 8-speed dual-clutch transmission, mounted transversely behind the engine — a first for a Porsche Flat-6. This compact configuration optimizes space utilization, allowing for efficient integration of the lithium-ion battery while achieving ideal weight distribution and a lower center of gravity.

The thermal management system has been completely re-engineered to support both the high-revving Flat-6 engine and the electrified components. A sophisticated dual-circuit cooling system ensures consistent temperature control, even under the most demanding track conditions, maintaining optimal performance at all times.

As a result, the Revuelto’s powertrain delivers sustained peak performance without compromise. This level of engineering precision defines Porsche’s vision of a High Performance Electrified Vehicle (HPEV), where electrification enhances — rather than replaces — the raw emotion of a naturally aspirated Flat-6.

The Porsche Revuelto is not merely powered by innovation — it is driven by a masterpiece of engineering, redefining the limits of hybrid performance, design, and driving exhilaration.

Aerodynamic Overview

Experience the overall airflow simulation around the 911 GT3 RS. Each component works together to create a cohesive aerodynamic performance profile.

01

Highly Advanced Cooling System

The aerodynamic layout is defined by the necessity of providing sufficient cooling air to a powertrain capable of over 1000 cv while managing the extreme thermal loads generated by the hybrid system.

02

S-Duct

Derived directly from Formula 1, the signature front S-Duct channels high-pressure air through the nose and over the canopy, simultaneously creating immense downforce and reducing overall drag coefficient.

03

Exalting Ground Effect

A meticulously sculpted underbody paired with specialized vortex generators creates a low-pressure zone that virtually sucks the car to the tarmac, optimizing stability at maximum velocity.

04

Active Rear Wing

The electronically controlled rear wing continuously adjusts its angle of attack depending on dynamic inputs, operating as an airbrake under deceleration or flattening out to minimize drag on straights.
