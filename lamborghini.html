The Raging Bull

Born from Rivalry

Ferruccio Lamborghini founded Automobili Lamborghini in 1963. Legend says the brand was born after a disagreement with Enzo Ferrari over car reliability (especially the clutch).

Ferruccio, already a successful tractor manufacturer, decided to build cars that were more refined and comfortable, yet extremely powerful. The result: a brand focused on bold design + extreme performance.

Headquarters

Every Lamborghini is still handcrafted in the historic Sant'Agata Bolognese factory. The facility includes advanced production lines, a dedicated carbon fiber research center, and state-of-the-art design labs.

Design Philosophy

Design Philosophy

The design language of Lamborghini was forever revolutionized by the Lamborghini Countach. Key elements include sharp angles, a low wedge-shaped profile, and an aggressive stance. This DNA continues today in the Aventador and the new hybrid Revuelto.

Engineering & Power

Lamborghini is evolving from purely naturally aspirated V12 engines into cutting-edge hybrid technology, combining raw power with modern electrification.

Brand Identity

Lamborghini represents aggression, drama, and extreme styling. Signature traits include:

  • Scissor doors (on V12 models)
  • Loud exhaust sound
